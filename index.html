About Me & Zero Sleep Studio

I'm KShalindu, an independent game developer based in Sri Lanka, fueled by a relentless passion for creating captivating interactive experiences. Under the moniker **Zero Sleep Studio**, I pour my heart and soul (and a fair bit of caffeine) into crafting games that stand out.

My Philosophy:

My core design philosophy revolves around **player agency, emergent gameplay, and polished mechanics.** I believe in building worlds that invite exploration and systems that encourage creative problem-solving. Every project is an opportunity to learn, innovate, and push the boundaries of what's possible with limited resources, focusing on delivering maximum fun per byte.

I specialize in immersive 3D games, from thrilling FPS experiences to expansive open-world concepts. Mobile performance optimization is a key area of expertise, ensuring my creations are accessible and enjoyable across a wide range of devices.

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll probably find me researching new game engines, experimenting with procedural generation, or, ironically, trying to catch up on some sleep!

Frequently Asked Questions

What game engine do you primarily use?

I primarily use Unity for both 2D and 3D game development due to its versatility and robust ecosystem. I also have some experience with Godot Engine.

Are your games available on PC or just mobile?

While most of my currently released games are targeted at Android, I develop with cross-platform compatibility in mind. Future projects may include PC releases.

Are you open to freelance work or collaborations?

Yes, I am open to discussing freelance opportunities and collaborations on interesting game development projects. Please reach out via the contact form or email!

How can I support your work?

Playing my games, leaving reviews, sharing them with friends, and following my social media channels are the best ways to support Zero Sleep Studio!
